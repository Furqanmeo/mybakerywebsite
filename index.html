Introduction of bakery

A bakery is an establishment that produces and sells flour-based baked goods made in an oven such as bread, cookies, cakes, doughnuts, bagels, pastries, and pies. Some retail bakeries are also categorized as caf√©s, serving coffee and tea to customers who wish to consume the baked goods on the premises.

Fresh Food: Bakeries provide fresh bread, pastries, and cakes, enhancing the quality of local food options. Local Employment: They create jobs within the community, contributing to economic growth. Social Hub: Bakeries often become gathering places for neighbors to meet and interact.

Baking is cooking of food by the action of dry heat in an oven. The degree of dryness of heat may be modified by the amount of steam produced from the items baked. Bread rolls, cakes, pastries, puddings etc.
